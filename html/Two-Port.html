## Two-Port Representation of the Active Antenna

We start with a general **ABCD two-port matrix** representation:

Since no current flows into the input, we obtain the condition:

\begin{equation} \frac{A}{B}=0 \end{equation}

For matching with 50 Ohm system impedance:

\begin{equation} \frac{D}{C}=Z_{0} \end{equation} \begin{equation} C=2.5 Cs \end{equation} \begin{equation} s=2 \pi f j \end{equation}

### Antenna Parameters

\begin{equation} Cs=3\cdot 10^{-12}\,\left[ \mathrm{F}\right] \end{equation} \begin{equation} Voc=0.25\,\left[ \mathrm{V}\right] \end{equation}

The output voltage is given by:

\begin{equation} V_{out}=2 \left(P R\right)^{0.5} \end{equation}

Numerical evaluation yields:

\begin{equation} V_{out}=0.4472\,\left[ \mathrm{V}\right] \end{equation} \begin{equation} V_{RMS}=0.316\,\left[ \mathrm{V}\right] \end{equation}

Finally, the input current into the 2-port is:

\begin{equation} I_{in}=C Voc + D I_{0} \end{equation}

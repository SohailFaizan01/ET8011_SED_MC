Additional Specifications

- Intermodulation products must be below -50 dBm in the frequency band of interest.

- CMOS18 technology must be used.

- The antenna must be protected against electrostatic discharge.

- Input referred noise must be below:

\begin{equation} S_{En}=1.0 \cdot 10^{-15} + \frac{0.001}{f^{2}}\,\left[ \mathrm{\frac{V^2}{m^2} Hz}\right] \end{equation}
